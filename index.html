  • All About Us

    It all started on the banks of Promenade beach in Pondicherry...sitting and sipping on an Irish Coffee and then the thought occurred...No that’s not how it began...

    Cafe Voyage was born out of the passion to Travel..to see and experience the world ; Exploring the cultural heritage of a destination, as much as observing and living the local lifestyle and most importantly FOOD. We picked up our favorite food from various destinations we love and put them all across our menu.

    We have travelled extensively and have our own list of Do’s and Dont's/likes and dislikes and will be more than happy to share them with you..whenever you walk in.. So come home to us to feel the essence of several different destinations on a plate.

  • Our Philosophy

    EAT Your Way

    Food is one of the biggest pleasures known to us. Imagine the number of ways a potato can be cooked/an onion can be sliced. The ingredients are the same; the only differences are the place and the lifestyle of the local people. We have tried to pick up some of these cooking styles and ingredients to give you the taste of the local dishes, to virtually transmit you to the lanes and bylanes of that destination, where the aroma leaves you waiting for that next time you are there..

    RELAX While you Dine

    To let you enjoy your food to the fullest, we have created a relaxed seating and homely environment at Cafe Voyage, so as to satisfy those hunger pangs at leisure. Indulge as much as you want to..for as long as you want to..

    Go Ahead And PLAN

    If you like any of those local dishes at our cafe and decide to pack your bags and head out to anywhere in the world, We are here to Help with our guidance!
